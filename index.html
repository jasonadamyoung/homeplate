Buster League

busterleague.outfielding.net: The Buster League (or "Original League") is a computer-simulation league that uses the Diamond Mind baseball program to simulate a 162-game season using the previous year's stats. There are 20 teams in the BusterLeague, 10 managed by the computer, and 10 managed by league participants.

Auction League

auctionleague.outfielding.net: The Auction League is also a computer-simulation league that uses the Diamond Mind baseball program to simulate a 162-game season using the previous year's stats. There are 8 teams in the AuctionLeague - all managed by league participants, playing head-to-head.
